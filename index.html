Daily Briefing

Your one-stop shop for weather, traffic, news, events, tweets, and pictures going on in your city of choice. As students of Rutgers Coding Bootcamp in Piscataway, NJ, Daily Briefing was created as a front-end project. HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, MaterializeCSS, and AJAX were used along with a Twitter widget and API's from The Dark Sky Forecast, Google Maps, Bing (for news), and Flickr.
